Welcome to BS Enterprises

7 Years Experience

BS Enterprises, located in Bharat Colony, Faridabad, is a premier construction firm with over 7 years of experience in the industry. Founded by Mr. Ashish Bawa and Mr. Yatendra Solanki, our company specializes in turnkey projects, offering comprehensive construction services ranging from residential and industrial to educational and commercial projects.

BS Enterprises is a premier construction firm dedicated to excellence in every project we undertake. With a steadfast commitment to quality and innovation, we strive to surpass expectations and deliver superior results. Our team of experienced professionals works tirelessly to ensure that each project is executed with precision and attention to detail. We believe in fostering strong relationships with our clients and are committed to providing exceptional service from start to finish. At BS Enterprises, we don't just build structures; we build trust, reliability, and a brighter future for all our stakeholders.
